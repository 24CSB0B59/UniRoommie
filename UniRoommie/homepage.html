About Us

Hostel life is an adventure—new friends, late-night talks, and shared experiences. But what happens when your "adventure" includes a roommate who blasts music while you study, turns the room into a laundry pile, or insists on waking up at 5 AM for a jog?

Finding the perfect roommate shouldn't feel like a gamble. That’s where UniRoomie comes in! By matching students based on sleep schedules, study habits, cleanliness, and social preferences, this tool ensures a smoother, more enjoyable hostel experience—so you can focus on making memories, not compromise.
